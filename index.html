CriptoSim

On CryptoSim users are able to recieve Signals for buying and selling cryptocurrencies, analyzes and compares signals by assigning robots to trade base on signals.

Project Vision

Our vision is to provide a platform which will lead our investors to potential increased profit” Cryptocurrency price prediction can provide a lending hand to cryptocurrency investors for making proper investment decisions in order to acquire higher profits while it can also support policy decision-making and financial researchers for studying cryptocurrency markets behavior

In this work, we evaluate the performance of advanced algorithms for predicting the price and movement of the three most popular cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH and XRP). The main contribution of this research lies in investigating two major questions: i) Are cryptocurrency prices a random walk process? ii) Is there a proper validation method of cryptocurrency price prediction models?

Project Plan

The platform will be suitable for both new and experienced investors and allow them to practice trading using paper trading before entering the real world trading

The goal is to gain investors trust and provide them with the most accurate data available. Cryptocurrency price prediction can provide a lending hand to cryptocurrency investors for making proper investment decisions in order to acquire higher profits while it can also support policy decision-making and financial researchers for studying cryptocurrency markets behavior.

The Restaurant Guide

When dining out, it's hard to choose among the many absurd restaurant choices. You spend hours browsing Yelp, Instagram, Wine & Dine, and finally choose a restaurant based on unreliable sources. The new restaurant scoring app puts an end to endless searches by creating a network of restaurant recommendations.

The goal of this application is to help users share, rate and comment their experience after dining in a restaurant so the next users have something to compare.

The target of this project is mostly the restaurant and their customers. The aim is to create a reliable source based on people’s experience and share them with others. This solution will help the restaurant with a good service to shine and have more customers and help the customers to have their meal in a good restaurant.
